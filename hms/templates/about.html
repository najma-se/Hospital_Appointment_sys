About SerenityCare General Hospital

Where compassion meets innovation in healthcare.

Welcome to SerenityCare

SerenityCare General Hospital is a premier healthcare institution committed to delivering patient-centered, technology-driven medical care. Established in 2010, SerenityCare has grown into a trusted name in the community, known for its compassionate staff, cutting-edge treatments, and commitment to continuous innovation.

Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible, efficient, and empathetic. We believe that healing is not just about treating illness — it's about nurturing the human spirit. Whether you visit for a routine checkup or a complex procedure, SerenityCare is here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Core Departments

General Medicine

Primary care physicians focusing on prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases.

Pediatrics

Compassionate care for infants, children, and adolescents with family-focused support.

Cardiology

Advanced heart care including diagnostics, monitoring, and non-invasive procedures.

Emergency & Trauma

24/7 emergency services with fast-response teams for critical and life-threatening conditions.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Comprehensive women’s health services including prenatal, maternity, and reproductive care.

Radiology & Imaging

State-of-the-art imaging diagnostics including X-ray, MRI, CT Scan, and ultrasound services.

Our Technology

SerenityCare leverages modern digital infrastructure like electronic health records (EHR), real-time appointment systems, and secure patient portals to ensure seamless interaction between patients and healthcare providers. With our HMS platform, patients can register online, view medical history, book appointments, and get timely reminders — all from the comfort of their home.

Our technology ensures accuracy, enhances safety, and minimizes administrative delays — allowing doctors and nurses to focus on what matters most: caring for you.
